Mehrangarh Fort

Mehrangarh Fort, located in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, is a grand structure built in the 15th century by Rao Jodha. It's one of the largest forts in India, perched on a hilltop 400 feet above the surrounding plains. Meaning "fort of the sun," it's a significant historical and architectural landmark.

Jaswant Thada

Jaswant Thada is a sublime testament to the amalgamation of Hindu and Mughal architectural brilliance. It is a temple-like structure bearing a central dome and four graceful corner domes, a unique architectural style that makes it stand out amongst the many structures of Rajasthan.

Clock Tower

A clock tower is a structure that houses a clock, typically with large, visible faces designed for public viewing, and often includes a mechanism for striking bells at the hours. These towers can be freestanding or integrated into a larger building, with some buildings incorporating clock faces directly onto their facades.
