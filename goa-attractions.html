Must-Visit Attractions

Baga Beach

Baga Beach, located in North Goa, India, is a popular and vibrant beach known for its lively atmosphere, water sports, and beautiful scenery. It's part of a long stretch of beach that connects to other famous beaches like Calangute and Anjuna. Baga Beach is a popular destination for both relaxation and adventure, offering a variety of activities and amenities.

Dudhsagar Falls

Dudhsagar Falls is perched at the top of a crescent shaped valley and surrounded by pristine forests, and it is amongst India's tallest waterfalls with a height of 310 m (1017 feet) and an average width of 30 metres (100 feet).

Basilica of Bom Jesus

The Basilica of Bom Jesus in Goa is a Baroque architectural masterpiece and the first minor basilica in India. Built between 1594 and 1605, it's renowned for housing the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier. This UNESCO World Heritage Site, part of the "Churches and Convents of Goa," showcases a unique blend of Indo-Portuguese styles.
