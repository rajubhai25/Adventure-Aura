Top Attractions

Gateway of India

The Gateway of India is a prominent arch monument located in Mumbai, India, built to commemorate King George V and Queen Mary's visit in 1911. It stands on the waterfront at Apollo Bunder and is a popular tourist attraction. The monument's construction was completed in 1924.

Marine Drive

Marine Drive in Mumbai is a famous 3.6-kilometer-long promenade located along the Arabian Sea, also known as the Queen's Necklace due to its stunning night view resembling a string of pearls. It's a popular spot for walks, watching sunsets, and enjoying the sea breeze. The area is known for its Art Deco architecture and includes notable buildings like New India Assurance, Eros Cinema, and the University of Mumbai.
