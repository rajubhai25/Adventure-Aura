Sonar Kila (Golden Fort)

Sonar Kila, also known as Jaisalmer Fort or the Golden Fort, is a historical fort located in Jaisalmer, Rajasthan, India. It's renowned for its golden sandstone walls, which give it a golden appearance, especially during sunset. The fort is a thriving, inhabited city within its walls, housing palaces, havelis, temples, and residences.

Patwon Ki Haveli

Patwon Ji ki Haveli is an interesting piece of Architecture and is the most important among the havelis in Jaisalmer. This is precisely because of two things, first that it was the first haveli erected in Jaisalmer and second, that it is not a single haveli but a cluster of 5 small havelis.

Bada Bagh

The name Bada Bagh translates to "Big Garden". Bada Bagh is a historic garden complex that features intricately carved cenotaphs dedicated to the Maharajas of the region. The cenotaphs were built by the Maharajas of the Jaisalmer State in the 18th, 19th, and early 20th centuries CE.
