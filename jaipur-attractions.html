Must-Visit Places

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal, also known as the Palace of Winds, is a five-story palace in Jaipur, India, built in 1799 by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh. Designed by Lal Chand Ustad, it's known for its striking architecture, particularly its 953 small windows (jharokhas) that allow cool air to flow through the building. The palace was intended as a viewing screen for royal women to observe daily life without being seen.

Amber Fort

Amber Fort, also known as Amer Fort or Amber Palace, is a historical fort in Amer, Rajasthan, India, located near Jaipur. It was commissioned by Maharaja Man Singh I in 1592 and served as the capital of the Kachhwaha Rajputs before Jaipur became the capital. The fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is known for its blend of Hindu and Mughal architectural styles, incorporating elements like intricate carvings, frescoes, and latticework.
