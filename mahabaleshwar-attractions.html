Venna Lake

Venna Lake is a popular tourist spot in Mahabaleshwar, Maharashtra, India, known for its scenic beauty and recreational activities. Constructed in 1842, it offers boat rides and horse rides, and is surrounded by trees and small eateries. It's a man-made lake originally built to supply water to the town, but now a popular destination for a weekend getaway.
