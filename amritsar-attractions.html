Famous Tourist Spots

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a prominent Sikh Gurdwara located in Amritsar, Punjab, India. It is the holiest site in Sikhism and a significant pilgrimage destination for Sikhs worldwide. The temple is built on a square platform in the middle of a sacred pool, known as Amrit Sarovar, and is known for its distinctive golden dome.

Wagah Border

The Wagah Border is the international border crossing between India and Pakistan, specifically located between Amritsar, India and Lahore, Pakistan. It's known for its daily "Beating the Retreat" ceremony, a flag-lowering ritual involving soldiers from both countries. This ceremony is a popular tourist attraction, showcasing a blend of national pride and camaraderie.
